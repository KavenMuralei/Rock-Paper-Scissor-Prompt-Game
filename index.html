What is Rock Paper Scissors?

Rock Paper Scissors is a hand game, usually played between two people, in which each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes by their hands. Players start each round by saying, “rock, paper, scissors, shoot!”. On “shoot,” each player holds out their hands out resembling the shapes of rock, paper and scissors. Rock crushes scissors, scissors cut paper, and paper covers rock. See if you have what it takes to beat the AI!
